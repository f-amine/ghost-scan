Frequently asked questions

How does the app process ID cards?

The app uses AI to detect, extract, and identify important information. It employs four sophisticated models: YOLO V7 for detecting and outlining ID cards, ResNet for cropping and straightening, DBNET for text detection, and SATRN for text recognition. Finally, Gemini Vision Pro is used for key information extraction.

What measures are taken to ensure user data privacy?

The app doesn't store any data from the ID cards. In fact, it doesn't use any database, ensuring complete data privacy and security for the user.

How accurate is the app in processing ID cards?

The app is tested on 150 ID cards, including real and generated ones, and has achieved an overall accuracy of 85%. The text recognition feature alone has a higher accuracy rate of 94%.

What aspect of the app impacts its accuracy the most?

The key information extraction part of the process is the most challenging and contributes to the variance in overall accuracy.

Is the app compatible with all versions of Moroccan ID cards?

Yes, the app is designed to be compatible with all versions of Moroccan ID cards, ensuring broad usability.

Can the app process ID cards in different conditions, like low light or wear and tear?

The app is engineered to handle various conditions, but optimal results are obtained with well-lit and reasonably maintained ID cards.

Is there any training required to use the app effectively?

The app is user-friendly and designed for ease of use, requiring minimal to no training for efficient operation.

Who can benefit from using this app?

The app is ideal for businesses and organizations that require quick and accurate data extraction from Moroccan ID cards, such as hotels, car rental services, and financial institutions.
